i am a STEM educator and activist committed to increasing access to technology and using the internet to create positive change in my community. through awesome programs like girls who code and hear me code, i have taught the basics of html, css, javascript, python, and APIs to over 100 women in the last year.

prior to teaching, i built tools and campaigns for non-profits at m+r and nrdc to help them raise money and make the world a better place. you can see my full resume here.

in my freetime, i volunteer with the dc abortion fund and femex. i also sing songs with jack on fire to let off steam and raise money for the causes i care about. i'm never opposed to a good board game, a new recipe, or an outdoor adventure. my hair changes color frequently.
